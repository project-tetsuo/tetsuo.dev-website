    Project Details¶

    Refer to MANIFESTO

    What we are

    • A way to deploy serverless without refactoring code.

    • Deploy direct from your repository.

    • No infrastructure to worry about.

    • Blindingly fast.

    What we are not

    • Yet another serverless DSL.

    • Lock-in to a platform.

    • A container technology.
